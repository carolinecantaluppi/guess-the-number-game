Guess The Number Game

Play the Game:

  1. Right-click anywhere on the web page and select Inspect or Inspect Element
  2. Go to the Console tab in the Developer Tools that open up. This is where you will see the game prompts and feedback
  3. Follow the instructions displayed on the web page to play the game.
  4. You will be prompted to guess a number, and the game will provide feedback on whether your guess is too high or too low until you guess the correct number or run out of tries.
